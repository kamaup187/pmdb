About KiotaPay

KiotaPay is a property management system that facilitates tenant allocation,billing, tenant payment and rent collection analysis. The system not only ensures that the landlord has every tool for automated operations but also provides a platform for the tenants to pay rent, utility,and request repairs from the comfort of their phones.

We free up time for the landlords to improve their facilities and engage in other important tasks. We do this by eliminating tedious and repetitive roles such as manual billing & reconciliation.

We believe that the ideal tenant values and appreciates time for family, work and other esteemed responsibilities as opposed to enduring long queues in banking halls and constant calls to the caretakers.
