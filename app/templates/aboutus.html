Our Company

Simplicity and Convenience

KiotaPay is a property management system that facilitates rent & amenities billing, and rent collection & analysis. The system not only ensures that the landlord has every tool for automated operations but also provides a platform for the tenants to pay rent, utility bills, and make requests from the comfort of their phones.

We free up time for the landlords to improve their facilities and engage in other important tasks. We do this by eliminating tedious and repetitive roles such as manual billing & reconciliation.
